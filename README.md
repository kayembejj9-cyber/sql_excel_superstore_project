SQL + Excel data analysis project on superstore.
DASHBOARD VIDEO
https://youtu.be/B7gwo2Ur8F0
 Business Context

The Superstore dataset represents a retail company selling products across different regions, categories, and customer segments.

The main goal of this analysis is to understand sales performance, profitability, and the impact of discounts on business results.


 Problem Statement

The company faces several challenges:
- Some high sales orders are not profitable
- Discounts may be affecting profit negatively
- It is unclear which categories and regions perform best



SQL Analysis

Using SQL, I filtered and analyzed the dataset:
- Sales above specific thresholds
- Profitability (positive vs negative profit)
- Regional performance comparison
- Discount impact on profit



 Key Insights

- High discounts are strongly associated with lower profit
- Some high sales orders generate losses
- Certain categories perform significantly better than others
- Profit varies greatly depending on region and customer behavior



Excel Dashboard

An interactive Excel dashboard was created including:
- KPI tracking (Sales, Profit, Orders, Discounts)
- Sales and profit trends over time
- Top 10 and Bottom 10 performance analysis
- Interactive filters (Region, Category, Segment)



 Conclusion

This analysis helps identify key business patterns and provides actionable insights to improve profitability and decision-making.
